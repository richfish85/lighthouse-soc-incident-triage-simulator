Lighthouse SOC — MVP Product Brief

Tagline: Navigate the noise.

Product concept

A lightweight SOC triage simulator that helps:

ingest security alerts
prioritize incidents
guide analyst response
track investigations
surface operational metrics

Think:

Splunk lite
TheHive lite
beginner-friendly SOC workflow trainer
MVP Goal

Simulate the workflow of a junior SOC analyst, not replicate an enterprise SIEM.

Success criteria:

User can:

✅ submit alert
✅ triage alert
✅ investigate alert
✅ update incident lifecycle
✅ see admin metrics

That is MVP.
